## Chapter 1: The Temporal Architecture (Leveling)

### The “Solo Mode Proficiency” System

Where Winds Meet gates progress via **Exploration Days** (your character age). Each real-world day after creation raises your potential cap *after* you clear that tier’s Breakthrough Trial. Days keep counting even if you stop logging in.

### What to Do While Capped

- **Oddities & Boundaries:** Turn-ins award Stored EXP and Exploration points.
- **Daily Commissions:** Four rotating tasks grant ≈8,000 EXP plus Commerce Coins; progress guild salary unlocks.
- **Outposts & Hideouts:** Reset every 30 minutes; perfect for banked EXP while farming weapons.
- **Life Skills:** Clinic, Scholar debates, and crafting ignore caps yet feed Stored EXP and permanent stats.
- **Story Prep:** Hoard turn-ins (leave quests at “Hand In”) so they cash out with +50% boosted EXP when cap rises.
- **Repeatable Focus:** Pause Main Story while capped; spam repeatables so payouts convert to Stored XP.

### Cap Slingshot Playbook

1. **During Cap:** Run side quests, exploration milestones, high-yield dailies. All EXP flows into Stored XP.
2. **Reset Eve:** Park Main Story objectives at “Turn In”. When the next tier unlocks, hand in for instant fresh EXP plus Stored release.
3. **Reset Day (First 2 Hours):** Log in at server day change to consume Stored XP at **+50% speed** while zones are empty. Expect 6–8 level jumps (e.g., 30 → 38) before the crowd catches up.
4. **Post-Slingshot:** Once the buffer empties, resume Main Story and repeat each cap window.

### Vitality Planning Beyond 50

- **Daily Pool:** 180 Vitality powers raids, bounty hunts, and many world bosses. Spend intelligently—no regen while capped.
- **Priority:** (1) Dreamscape raid lockouts, (2) Sect drills, (3) Bounty Boards, (4) Elite hunts. Do low-priority outposts when Vitality hits zero.
- **Weekly Reset:** Wednesday 04:00 server. Sync your Wednesday market flip (see Chapter 3) with raid scheduling so you can trade between groups.
- **No Cash Skip:** Vitality refills can’t be bought; only rare event items restore small amounts. Plan around scarcity.

### Breakthrough Trials & Cooldowns

- Every tier needs a **Solo Mode Breakthrough** (timed duel). Later fights (40 → 50+) expect upgraded gear, Mystic Arts, and tight parries.
- Some tiers impose a 24h lockout after failure. Attempt near daily reset to retry immediately.
- Story quests often pause until you break through; spend downtime on exploration XP, Oddities, life skills, and Stored XP farming.

### Stored XP = 150% Catch-Up, Not Instant Levels

<div class="callout research">
<span class="callout-title">Research Note — Stored XP Mechanics</span>
EXP earned at cap is banked as **Stored EXP**. When the cap rises, you consume it at **+50% bonus** while earning fresh EXP. It isn’t an instant dump—you still play to drain the pool.
</div>

- **Stay active at cap:** Oddities, outposts, side quests, daily commissions all preload Stored XP.
- **Slingshot window:** First 2–3 hours post-reset are the calmest if you’ve banked EXP.
- **Diminishing returns:** Stored XP has no cap, but grinding far past the limit yields smaller gains once the +50% buffer ends.

### Level 50+: Horizontal Power Systems

Dreamscape (Lv50) shifts the game from raw levels to systems mastery:

1. **Weapon Tuning:** Unlocks at 50 for Purple/Gold weapons. Hoard Tuning Stones from Day 1; reroll only on long-term pieces.
2. **Armory Resonance:** Slot legacy weapons/armor for permanent nodes. Don’t salvage high-roll items—embed them.
3. **Set Bonuses:** Coherent 4-piece combos (Rainwhisper/Moonflare for tanks, Hawking/Eaglerise for DPS) beat mismatched iLvl.
4. **Vitality Economy:** Raids, bounties, elite hunts now consume Vitality. Plan runs so you never hit zero before priority content.

## Chapter 2: Phase 1 — The Filter (Hours 0–2)

### Character Creation Optimization

#### Aspiration Talents: The Math

- **Drunken Precision:** Only affects “Pitch Pot”. Irrelevant for combat.
- **Rhetoric Edge (Mandatory):** Grants **+2 Inspiration**.
- **Why:** Early debates have tight stat checks. Failures force combat with worse rewards. Rhetoric Edge is the only mathematically viable speedrun choice.

#### Social Preference & Starting Mode

- **Shared Journey (Recommended):** Drops you into the online shard instantly. You can still lock lobbies but keep public bosses and announcements.
- **Solo Journey:** Instanced version of the map. Friends join only if invited. Use for spoiler-free exploration or benchmarking.
- **Switching Later:** Toggle via the World icon after level 10. Mode changes don’t affect Stored XP, sect standing, or story.

#### World Mode & Difficulty Locks

- **Solo vs. Shared:** Only sets your initial shard. Swap anytime via the world menu.
- **Legend Difficulty:** Opt-in at creation only. **Legend changes loot tables**, front-loading blue drops and prestige cosmetics. You can downgrade later, but you can’t return to Legend—stick with it through the prologue at least.
- **Recommended / Expert:** Default flexible choices. Swap once the prologue ends.

#### UX Configuration: ARPG Mode

- Switch from “MMORPG Mode” to **“ARPG Mode”** immediately.
- **Reason:** Locks camera to mouse vector for rapid 180° turns and back-attack counters.
- **Bonus:** Disable combat time-stop assists in Settings → Combat once parry timing feels natural.

#### Interface Cleanup Checklist

- **Detailed Exploration Guidance:** Settings → Interface → Exploration Tips = Detailed to show chest/Oddity icons.
- **Keybind Tweaks:** Rebind “Swap Weapon” (R) if you use controllers—Mouse 4 is popular for stance swaps.
- **Combat Prompts:** Turn off “Cinematic Time Stop” past Lv20 to match raid cadence.
- **Wind Sense Shortcut:** Bind Wind Sense to an easy mouse button; you’ll spam it for Oddities.

## Chapter 3: Phase 2 — The Skill Acquisition Matrix

These skills are “soft-locked” behind puzzles. Missing them leaves you non-viable for mid-game content.

### 1. General’s Shrine — Mobility

**Location:** North of Heaven’s Pier.

<div class="callout research">
<span class="callout-title">Research Note — Target Level & Prep</span>
Arrive around Lv18–22 with at least one upgraded weapon. Bring antidotes; the shrine tournament sprays poison if you fail parries.
</div>

#### Cloud Steps (Double Jump)

- **Quest Hook:** During “Echoes in the Shrine,” examine cracked tiles on the west side of the statue plaza.
- **Execution:** Perform a **Plunging Attack** (Jump + Heavy) to shatter the floor and drop into the hidden chamber.
- **Reward:** Cloud Steps manual in the basement chest; hold Space for a second jump or downward kick.
- **Usage:** Essential for high ledges, dodging large AoEs, and traversal puzzles (Five-Tone Bells, mausoleums).

#### Lion’s Roar (AoE CC)

Requires ringing 4 Hidden Bells across Qinghe (one deep in Witherwilds).

- **Utility:** 14-hit roar that staggers mobs, detonates certain DoTs, and grants a short damage-reduction buff. Costs 30 Vitality—treat as a panic button.
- **Cooldown:** ≈15 seconds baseline. Upgrade to Tier 3 ASAP for longer stun/damage reduction.
- **Bell Locations:** (i) West wall inside shrine compound (near Cloud Steps breach). (ii) Southwest hillside toward Stonewash Strand. (iii) Northeast cliff with wooden overlook above Buddha Fort. (iv) South of Wraith Village teleport in Witherwilds (sprint past enemies if under-leveled).

<div class="callout warning">
<span class="callout-title">Warning — Bell #4 Survival</span>
Activate the Wraith Village waypoint before attempting the Witherwilds bell. Pop a movement pill, spam Cloud Steps, ignore combat; the bell interaction takes under two seconds.
</div>

### 2. Northern Vow Ruins — Stealth

**Reward:** Touch of Death (Assassination).

<div class="callout research">
<span class="callout-title">Research Note — Entry Checklist</span>
Level 20+, blue weapon, 2 antidotes, Veil of Stillness equipped. Courtyard archers hit hard—pull with a bow.

#### “Literacy Check” Puzzles

1. **Courtyard:** Clear boss Zhang Bao (Lv20). Loot the *Map Clue* and light only red-marked braziers. Plunge the center seal to open stairs.
2. **Wall Text Puzzle:** Push incorrect characters—left wall block furthest from entrance; right wall blocks 3 and 5.
3. **Waterfall:** Climb the statue in the underground hall, drop into river path, go behind waterfall to clear jammed water wheel. Reveals the golden chest with Touch of Death.
4. **Final Chamber:** Slot Touch of Death in your Mystic Art wheel; it passively prompts when an enemy is unaware.

<div class="callout pro-tip">

Touch of Death auto-prompts on unaware humanoids while Veil of Stillness is active. It trivializes camp clears and infiltration quests—slot it immediately.

### 3. Stonewash Strand — The Raid Key

<div class="callout warning">
<span class="callout-title">Warning — Raid Viability Check</span>
A raid team without **Meridian Touch** fails 100% of the time vs. Dalang Phase 2.
</div>

**Skill:** Meridian Touch (Freeze)

- **Quest:** Talk to Yan Qiren near the petrified villagers south of General’s Shrine to start “Meridian Touch.” Clear the mini-dungeon to learn it.
- **Mechanic:** Close-range acupoint strike that freezes most elites/bosses mid-ultimate. Costs 40 Vitality, ~30s cooldown—coordinate casts.
- **Usage Tips:** Stay in melee during gold-glow telegraphs to react instantly. Works on Qianye’s scream, Dalang’s Berserk, and several wipe mechanics.
- **Raid Etiquette:** Assign numbers (“Meridian 1/2”) at start. Casting simultaneously wastes the second use but still consumes Vitality.

### 4. Utility Skills

- **Celestial Seize (Telekinesis):** Start “Foul Play” with Qiu Yuehai at Heaven’s Pier market wall. Pull distant chests, disarm bandits mid-swing, or steal quest items through windows. Theft in sight of guards raises Crime.
- **Tai Chi:** During “Another New Wing,” observe (don’t kill) the bear reaching for honey west of the shrine. Tai Chi manipulates heavy mechanisms and provides short-range CC.

#### Skill Synergy Notes

- **Leaping Toad + Lion’s Roar:** Leap in, roar for stun, detonate while enemies remain locked.
- **Wolflike Frenzy + Free Morph (PvP):** Chain Frenzy into a morph to reset animations and catch escaping players.
- **Yaksha Rush + Free Morph (PvP):** Same concept—morph cancels recovery for guaranteed hits.
- **Cloud Steps Gap Close:** Keep Cloud Steps slotted even late game; it’s the fastest way to close distance before Mystic Arts like Soaring Spin or Thousand Wave Flute.

## Chapter 4: Phase 3 — Economic Arbitrage

### The “Wednesday” Pump and Dump

| Day | Price Index | Strategy |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Sat–Sun | 80–100% | **Accumulate.** Buy at base price. |
| Mon–Tue | 120–200% | **Hold.** Resist early selling. |
| **Wednesday** | **300% (Peak)** | **Liquidate everything.** |
| Thu–Fri | 40–60% | **Avoid.** Market correction phase. |

### City Access: Beating the Gatekeeper

- **Boss Requirement:** Chapter 1 finale vs. Qianye (Lv25) on the Kaifeng bridge. Defeat her and reach ~Lv32.
- **Smuggler Route:** After the fight, head to **Restless Docks** east of Heaven’s Pier. Talk to **Old Zhang** → “I need to get to the city quietly” to enter Kaifeng via sewers.
- **No Skip:** Swimming or spamming the boat earlier teleports you back. Finish the chapter, then use the boat to skip bridge guards later.

### Commerce Coins vs. Standard Coins

- **Coins (Ingot icon):** Repairs, vendor purchases, basic consumables.
- **Commerce Coins (Scales icon):** Earned via trading, guild salary, Cat Treasury. Used for housing rent, high-end trade goods, guild tech.
- Track both separately; the Wednesday flip primarily yields **Commerce Coins**.

### Currency Hierarchy & Spending Rules

| Currency | Source / Usage | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Echo Beads | Premium (top-up only) | 160 per premium pull. Prioritize Monthly Pass (stack to 180 days) before raw purchases; buy QoL unlocks instead of gambling. |
| Echo Jades | Exploration rewards + first top-up | Also 160 per pull. Save first 7,000 for Morale Chant pages/notes (Chapter 5) before touching gacha. |
| Commerce Coins | Weekly Season shop (2,500 cap) or trade flips | Costs 20,000 Jade Fish to hit the weekly cap. Spend on Chicken Noodles, housing rent, guild tech, rare merchants. |
| Coins | Universal drops | Daily repairs, consumables, teleport fees. Don’t drain this on cosmetics. |

Commerce Coins from the Season shop cost 50 Jade Fish each. Maxing the 2,500 weekly cap requires 20,000 Jade Fish—plan the grind prior to Wednesday flips.

### Premium Store Must-Buys

- **Quick Feet (300 Beads):** Permanent sprint buff plus slide skill for Guild Wars/exploration.
- **Spirited Courser Mount (1,280 Beads):** One-tap gathers every resource in a 40 m radius (excludes chests). Pairs with Chicken Noodles farming.
- **Snow Oath Mount (60 Beads):** Auto-loots enemy drops when “Follow” is toggled. Slower than manual looting but low attention cost.

### Chicken Noodles Resource Loop

- **Unlock NPC:** Niu Mang in Kaifeng (near the chess boy). Buy Wheat Rice first to unlock the Chicken Noodle recipe.
- **Cost:** 50 Commerce Coins per bowl.
- **Buff:** Grants Level Harvesting 2 for 10 minutes (+20% herbal crit rate). Non-stackable.
- **Infinite Farm:** Eat noodles → resource sweep → wait 5 minutes (online respawn timer) → repeat within buff duration.
- **Synergy:** Combine with Spirited Courser for 40 m instant gathers; run in online shards to leverage global respawns.

### Trade Workflow (Expanded Wednesday Flip)

1. Visit the **Kaifeng Trade Office** (Southern Gate Avenue) every Saturday to buy up to 90 local goods while the market is “Closed” (80–100% price).
2. Inventory slots limit profit. Rent storage (future housing) or invest in guild trade skills for carry capacity.
3. Hold through Monday/Tuesday. Sell everything on Wednesday when prices average 250–300%. Remaining goods **expire** at Saturday reset.
4. Foreign goods (fixed 100%) are for cross-world selling; skip until you understand Cong Bushao tax rules.

#### Trading Logistics

- **Entry NPCs:** Zhang Qiyue (Local Goods) + Zhao Weng (Foreign Goods) inside the Trade Office. Talk to Zhang first for fluctuating inventory.
- **Weekly Limit:** 180 Local Goods per character (90-item Saturday batch + 90-item Monday restock).
- **Expiration:** Goods spoil at 04:00 Saturday server—unsold cargo vanishes with no refund.
- **Commerce vs. Coins:** Local flips award Commerce Coins only. Keep Coin reserves for repairs.
- **Tax Optimization:** Selling inside your guild base (Cong Bushao) cuts tax up to 15% if guild unlocked trade tech.
- **Foreign Goods:** Save for cross-world visits. Hop into a friend’s shard and sell at their Trade Office for fixed 180%. Inventories track separately.

### Unlocking Flight: Afterglow Pendant & Fan Glider

- **Requirement:** Reach Qinghe Exploration Rank 4 (~4,000 points) to receive the **Afterglow Pendant**. Equip it in your accessory slot; flight skills are disabled without it.
- **How to Reach Rank 4:** Light all Stone Lanterns (Afterglow Jianghu Legacy), collect Golden Toad Oddities, activate Boundary Markers, solve shrines/puzzles (Five-Tone Bells, mausoleums).
- **Fan Glider:** Unlocks at Rank 5. Hold Space while airborne to glide; chain with Cloud Steps and Meteor Flight.
- **Region Specific:** Each region (Qinghe, Kaifeng, etc.) has its own flight unlock.

#### Rank 4 Sprint Plan

1. Activate all 15 Boundary Markers in Qinghe for ~900 points.
2. Complete “Afterglow” Jianghu Legacy: talk to Fisherman Qiu at dusk, light 8 lanterns, finish pot-hopping microtrial for Meteor Flight (+600 points).
3. Collect 9 Golden Toad statues along riverbanks (Wind Sense to locate). Each grants 80 points + an Oddity turn-in.
4. Clear Taiping Mausoleum and Five-Tone Bells puzzles for another 800+ points.
5. Claim the Pendant from Map → Exploration once you cross 4,000 points, then equip it in the Relic slot.

## Chapter 5: Phase 4 — Multiplayer & Sects

### Multiplayer Toggle & Co-op Basics

- **Unlock:** Reach Level 10, then click the World Mode icon to swap Solo ↔ Shared Journey. Host up to 3 friends in Solo worlds; online shards support 10-player activities.
- **Progress Handling:** Hosts keep story progression. Helpers earn **Adventure Slips** + loot but don’t alter their own story.
- **Matchmaking:** Use Fellowship → Instance tab for Dalang raid, world bosses, or PvP queues even while in Solo.

### Raid Heuristics — Dalang (Horse Tamer)

Requires a “Trinity” comp: 1 Spear tank, 1 Umbrella/Fan healer.

- **Phase 1 Signal:** Horse glows **red** → two players must **parry simultaneously**. Single parry = damage taken.
- **Phase 2 Signal:** Dalang glows **gold** and screams → **Meridian Touch** immediately.

### Sect Affiliation (v9.0)

Joining a Sect unlocks Inner Arts, cosmetics, and daily drills.

- **How to Join:** Around Lv30, the Sect Envoy in Kaifeng sends invites. Complete each sect’s duel/task to enter.
- **Core Sects (Global Launch):**
  - **Raging Tides Hall:** Spear/water tanks; aggro tools & bubble shields.
  - **Hollow Vale:** Umbrella/occult healers; HoT boosts and cleanses.
  - **Midnight Blades (CN reference, pending global):** Twin-blade assassins with stealth bursts.
  - **Sunset Prayer / Archer orders (future patch):** Bow/umbrella hybrids emphasizing ranged DPS.
- **Daily Life:** Complete sect commissions, donate materials, vote in monthly leadership elections to unlock higher-rank Inner Arts and exclusive outfits.
- **Switching:** Possible but wipes contribution—choose carefully.

#### Joining Process & Daily Loop

1. **Unlock Invite:** Finish Chapter 1 + reach Lv30. Sect Envoy spawns in Kaifeng’s Scholar Quarter.
2. **Trial Examples:** Raging Tides forces you to tank waves without dropping below 50% HP; Hollow Vale asks you to keep NPCs alive via umbrella heals.
3. **Contribution Sources:** Daily drills (3/day), Sect Bounties, donation of crafted goods, Sect Duels. Contribution unlocks shops and inner art pages.
4. **Leadership Elections:** Weekly influence votes select Captains who launch joint raids or set sect buffs. Align with active communities if you plan to lead.

#### Sect Playbooks

**Raging Tides Hall (Tank / Water Spear)**

- **Signature Skills:** Tidal Wall (bubble shield), Tidesurge Pull, Leviathan’s Gaze taunt.
- **Weapon Pairings:** Stormbreaker Spear + guardian sword off-hand. Stack HP/DEF nodes in Inner Meridian grid.
- **Set Synergy:** 4pc Rainwhisper + 4pc Moonflare for shield uptime/damage reduction. Prioritize HP% Armory relics.
- **Trial Tip:** Equip Lion’s Roar to control adds during the induction gauntlet.

**Hollow Vale (Occult Healer / Umbrella)**

- **Signature Skills:** Wraith Blossom HoT, Soul Lantern cleanse, Panacea Fan turrets.
- **Weapon Pairings:** Soulshade Umbrella + Fan (support) or Umbrella + Sword (battle cleric). Stack Elegance/Spirit via Clinic quests.
- **Set Synergy:** 4pc Moonflare (HoT amplification) + 2pc Serene Feathers (CDR). Consider Tailor career to craft missing pieces.
- **Trial Tip:** Bring 2 AoE heals and pre-place Panacea turrets before waves spawn; NPC deaths reset the trial.

**Midnight Blades (Assassin / Twin Blades)**

- **Status:** Playable globally once “Shadow Accord” patch drops (ETA winter 2025); currently preview.
- **Signature Skills:** Shadowstep triple dash, Hemorrhage Chains, Veil Refresh on kill.
- **Build Goals:** Max Crit Rate/DMG, leverage Touch of Death opener, pair Hawking + Eaglerise sets.
- **Trial Tip:** Puzzle segment requires stealth routes without alarms. Practice Celestial Seize to pull keys silently.

**Sunset Prayer / Archer Orders**

- **Status:** Future patch (referenced in codex). Ranged umbrella/bow hybrids.
- **Focus:** Long-duration DoTs, battlefield control, teamwide crit buffs.
- **Prep:** Level Bow proficiency even if you main melee—the sect requires accuracy trials with headshot goals.

## Chapter 6: Phase 5 — Systems Mastery (Professions, Guilds, PvP)

### Life Skills & Careers

- **Evercare Clinic (Healer Career):** Unlock in Jiangnan after Lv25 quest. Treat patients daily to raise Constitution, unlock stronger draughts, and gain Medicinal Tale stat boosts.
- **Scholar / Debater:** Visit Cloudrise Library in Kaifeng. Debates grant Inspiration, Inner Way points, and shop discounts. Synergizes with Rhetoric Edge.
- **Crafts (Blacksmith, Tailor, Chef, Carpenter):** Each career has its own workbench + weekly cap. Blacksmithing pushes weapons beyond +5; Tailoring crafts set fillers; Cooking provides raid banquets; Carpentry (future) makes furniture.
- **Career Tips:** Rotate daily tasks to stay within merit caps, stockpile materials pre-reset, prioritize Clinic/Scholar—they yield direct combat stats.

#### Evercare Clinic Macro

1. Unlock “Evercare Needs Hands” in Jiangnan (Chapter 2) and choose the Healer career at intake.
2. Diagnose 3 patients daily (pulse reading, herbal mixing mini-games). Successful diagnosis awards Constitution +0.5.
3. Craft tiered **Medicinal Tales**; turn in collected notes for permanent ATK/HP bonuses.
4. Upgrade clinic rank weekly by submitting herbs from Witherwilds or trade. Higher rank unlocks better draught recipes.
5. **Why it matters:** Constitution is a hidden stat that raises max HP/resistances; neglecting clinic leaves you 10–15% squishier by Dreamscape.

#### Scholar / Debate Circuit

- **Unlock:** Cloudrise Library in Kaifeng after Lv25. Talk to Debate Registrar for Scholar badge.
- **Loop:** Complete debate challenges (logic mini-game) for Inspiration + Inner Way points. Daily debates reward Study Notes for shop discounts and sect influence.
- **Debate Gear:** Equip accessories with Elegance or Wit stats; they influence debate outcomes.
- **Meta Use:** Bank Inspiration to pass hard persuasion checks instead of fighting, saving time/resources.

#### Crafting Careers Overview

- **Blacksmith:** Enhances weapons past +5, rerolls substats, forges duplicates for Armory Resonance. Weekly merit cap: 1,200.
- **Tailor:** Crafts armor fillers and cosmetics. Essential for finishing set bonus gaps when RNG fails.
- **Chef:** Prepares banquets (+5% party ATK/DEF for 30 min). Stockpile before raids; food stacks with potions.
- **Carpenter (Preview):** Will craft furniture granting storage slots and passive buffs once housing patch drops. Hoard timber now.
- **Workflow Tip:** Use gathering careers (Herbalist, Hunter) to feed materials into crafting alts. Each career has a daily order board—do the ones labeled “Merit Bonus” first.

### Inner Arts & Buildcraft

<a id="chapter-innerways"></a>

- **Inner Meridian Grid:** Spend Inner Way points (quests, debates, sect drills) to unlock talents like *Drunken Poet* (crit sustain) or *Ironheart* (damage reduction). Plan routes around your weapon + sect.
- **Weapon + Sect Pairings:**
  - Spear + Raging Tides + Rainwhisper set = premier tank (shield uptime, taunts, mitigation).
  - Umbrella + Hollow Vale + Moonflare set = raid healer (Panacea turrets, HoT boosts).
  - Twin Blades + Midnight Blades + Hawking set = stealth burst for PvP/PvE.
- **Armory Resonance:** Slot past weapons aligned with your build to gain passive ATK/DEF multipliers—think of it as a set bonus bank.

#### Planning the Meridian Grid

1. **Map Your Role:** Decide early (tank, healer, DPS). This determines which Meridian rings you prioritize (Heart for healers, Spine for tanks, Blade for DPS).
2. **Point Income:** Story quests, Scholar debates, Sect drills, PvP placements grant weekly Inner Way points. Track them for high-cost nodes (12+ points).
3. **Route Examples:**  
   • **Tank:** Ironheart → Mountain Root → Tidal Bulwark  
   • **Healer:** Gentle Rain → Verdant Pulse → Spirit Bloom  
   • **Assassin:** Drunken Poet → Flowing Shadows → Bloodied Crescendo
4. **Respecs:** Free up to Lv40 via Inner Arts UI, then cost materials. Screenshot your grid before experimenting.
5. **Synergy Check:** Align Armory nodes with Meridian nodes (e.g., slot high-HP weapons if you pumped HP nodes). This multiplicative layering lets optimized players survive raid one-shots.

#### Morale Chant Acquisition Route

1. **Qinghe Merchant Tang Bao (Moonveil Mountain):** Buy 10× “Morale Chant: Page” at 500 Echo Jades each (total 5,000). Unlocks base Inner Way.
2. **Kaifeng Merchant Tang Lun / Weiyang Stall:** Buy 10× “Morale Chant Notes” at 200 Echo Jades each (total 2,000) to upgrade the Inner Way.
3. **Optional Shop Clear:** Full inventory costs 14,800 Jades. Prioritize Morale Chant before gacha banners—the Inner Way grants stacking penetration, Attack%, and Heal% up to five stacks.

#### Inner Way Management Tips

- **Lock Core Pages:** Mark your four main Inner Ways to avoid accidental recycling.
- **Recycle the Rest:** Convert unused pages into currency via Tips Exchange, then buy the 600/week random-note boxes tied to your martial art.
- **Schedule Purchases:** Align Jade spending with server resets to grab weekly boxes on cooldown.

#### Combat Execution & Qi Management

- Don’t instantly execute enemies when their Qi breaks. Keep hitting to squeeze extra DPS while they’re immobilized.
- Save executions for new add waves or when you need invulnerability frames; the damage window is usually more valuable.

### Guilds & Factions

- **Joining:** After reaching Kaifeng, speak to the Guild Envoy on the main avenue to choose one of three NPC-backed guilds: **Bladesworn** (combat), **Changle Pavilion** (trade), **Farshore Scholars** (exploration/crafting). Player guilds sit under these umbrellas.
- **Benefits:** Daily Commerce Coin salary, contribution unlocking guild tech (tax breaks, combat buffs), access to Cong Bushao (reduced-tax merchant), guild quests (caravans, boss hunts), communal facilities (dummies, storage).
- **Switching:** Possible but includes cooldown + rank loss.
- **Guild vs. Sect:** Guilds = economic/social; sects = combat specialization. Engage with both.

#### Guild Enrollment Flow

1. Clear the Kaifeng entry quest, then interact with the Recruit Board near the plaza.
2. Review guild focus/passives (e.g., Bladesworn grants +2% damage vs elites per rank).
3. Submit application or create your own guild (cost: 10,000 Coins). Custom guilds still align to an umbrella for tech trees.
4. Complete onboarding tasks (deliver supplies, sparring) to earn your first rank and unlock salary.

#### Daily/Weekly Activities

- **Guild Salary:** Paid at 20:00 server if you earn 100+ contribution that day. Missed window = forfeited salary.
- **Caravan Escorts:** Evening events yielding major contribution; success unlocks extra trade goods for everyone.
- **Guild Hunt:** Instanced boss targeted by guildmates; heavy source of Armory fodder + tech currency.
- **Research Tech:** Officers allocate pooled contribution into nodes (Trade Tax Reduction, Combat Potions). Check the tech tab weekly to ensure your contributions fuel relevant upgrades.

### PvP & Crime 101

- **Duel/Arena:** Toggle Shared Journey, then challenge via Social wheel or Kaifeng Arena NPC for structured 1v1–5v5. Rewards include currency and ranking cosmetics.
- **Unending Battle (Battle Royale):** Menu queue unlocked around Lv40. Drops exclusive titles and fashion currency.
- **Open-World PK:** Enabling PK or attacking innocents raises **Notoriety**. High Notoriety spawns bounty hunters or lets players cage you via Pardon Parade.
- **Avoiding PvP:** Stay Solo or keep PK toggle off. Even in Shared Journey you must manually opt into Crime status—accidental hits in sanctuaries only warn you.

#### Mode Breakdown

- **Arena Ladder:** Weekly seasons; earn Arena Seals for cosmetics or PvP-stat gear. Formats include Best-of-3 duels and 3v3 elimination.
- **Unending Battle:** 60-player drop-in with shrinking safe zone. Loot skills from defeated players. Top 5 placements grant Battle Laurels for certain Inner Art nodes.
- **Faction Skirmishes:** Guild-triggered rivalries over control points for trade buffs.

#### Crime & Bounty System

- **Notoriety Tiers:** 0 (Lawful) → 100 (Wanted) → 500 (Infamous). Higher tiers = longer jail/cage timers when caught.
- **Pardon Parade:** Player-run bounty hunts. Captured players are caged for 5 minutes unless guildmates pay bail.
- **Decay:** Notoriety decays by 10 per real hour while online in Solo mode. Logging off pauses decay—find safe AFK spots.
- **Civilian Safety:** Sanctuaries/story instances disable PK entirely. If griefed, swap to Solo; aggressors can’t follow.

### Advanced Exploration Hits

- **Five-Tone Bells:** Audio puzzle on Qinghe cliffs. Reward: Lightness Skill upgrade.
- **Taiping Mausoleum Riddles:** Hidden dungeon (~Lv30) with large Exploration XP + Antique drops.

#### Taiping Mausoleum Entry

- Solve three stone riddles outside (answers on nearby tablets).
- Inside, use Tai Chi to rotate statues and align light beams. Keep Meridian Touch ready—the guardian enrages if puzzles drag.
- Reward: Antique sets, Exploration XP, hidden Armory node blueprint.

#### Ghost Market Rumor Chain

1. Collect rumors from Kaifeng taverns (NPCs with “…” at night).
2. Complete “Lantern of Dusk” side quest to earn an invitation.
3. Visit the Ghost Market between 21:00–23:00 server near riverside warehouses. Vendors sell unique oddities/dyes for Commerce Coins.

#### Global Caution List

- **Pillow Powder:** Save until max level so drops scale to the highest tier before activating the buff.
- **Gear Investment:** Low-level gear is disposable. Don’t dump Tuning Stones or high-tier upgrades into pre-50 items.
- **PvP Reality:** Modes aren’t normalized. Avoid open-world PK until you meet level/gear thresholds.
- **Exploration Value:** Oddities and chests drive character stats—skipping them leaves you behind despite equal gear.
- **Bears & Tai Chi:** Don’t auto-kill bears. Many puzzles require using Tai Chi on bears to break rocks/open chests.
- **Starter Weapons:** Nameless Sword + Healing Fan carry you to max level; use the tutorial crate for rarer weapons instead.
